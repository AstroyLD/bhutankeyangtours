Discover Bhutan
Your Guide to Unforgettable Experience

Cultures & Traditions

Immerse yourself in the rich and vibrant culture of Bhutan. Discover a land deeply rooted in tradition, where ancient customs and beliefs are still alive today. Witness the daily rituals, explore the remarkable monasteries, and engage with the warm and welcoming locals. Experience a way of life that has remained virtually unchanged for centuries, and gain a profound understanding of Bhutanese culture and tradition

Festivals

Step into a world of vibrant colors, rhythmic dances, and mesmerizing rituals. Experience the magic of Bhutan's festivals, where locals and visitors come together in joyful celebration. From the iconic Paro Tshechu to the colorful Punakha Dromchoe, these festivals offer an unforgettable glimpse into Bhutanese traditions and spirituality. Immerse yourself in the festive spirit, witness ancient mask dances, and revel in the cultural extravaganza that brings the country to life

National Dress

Embark on a journey to discover Bhutan's national dress, the gho for men and the kira for women. These traditional garments are more than just clothing; they represent a deep sense of pride and identity. Learn about the intricate weaving techniques, the vibrant patterns, and the symbolic significance of each element. Try on the gho or kira yourself, and experience the beauty and elegance of Bhutan's traditional attire.

Biodiversity

Uncover the astonishing biodiversity of Bhutan, a hidden gem nestled in the Eastern Himalayas. With its diverse ecosystems ranging from subtropical jungles to high alpine meadows, Bhutan is a sanctuary for a wide array of plants and animals. Explore the lush forests, spot rare and endangered species, and marvel at the breathtaking vistas. Discover a country that prides itself on its commitment to conservation and preservation of its awe-inspiring biodiversity.

Sports

Experience the thrill of traditional Bhutanese sports and games. From archery, the national sport, to khuru, a dart-throwing game, get ready to challenge yourself in ancient disciplines played for centuries. Engage with friendly locals during friendly competitions, and learn about the cultural significance of these traditional sports. Immerse yourself in the exhilarating atmosphere, and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Treks & Tours

Embark on an unforgettable adventure through Bhutan's rugged landscapes and pristine wilderness. Explore the majestic Himalayan peaks, traverse scenic mountain passes, and delve into remote valleys untouched by modernity. Whether you prefer gentle walks or challenging treks, there is a trail for everyone. Experience the unique blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and spiritual sanctity that make Bhutan a dream destination for trekkers and nature enthusiasts. Let our expert guides lead you through this enchanting land, ensuring an immersive and unforgettable experience.

Choose Your Destination

Haa

Welcome to Haa, a picturesque dzongkhag nestled in the western part of Bhutan. Known for its serene beauty and cultural heritage, Haa offers a perfect escape for nature enthusiasts and those seeking tranquility. With its stunning landscapes, pristine lakes, and lush green valleys, Haa is a paradise for hikers and adventurers. Immerse yourself in the local traditions and explore historical sites like Lhakhang Karpo and Lhakhang Nagpo. Discover the unique cuisine, warm hospitality, and rich culture that make Haa a must-visit destination in Bhutan.

Paro

Step into Paro, a charming dzongkhag located in the heart of Bhutan. Renowned for its iconic Tiger's Nest Monastery, Paro is a treasure trove of natural beauty and cultural heritage. The stunning Paro Valley, surrounded by majestic mountains, offers breathtaking views that will leave you captivated. Explore ancient temples, visit traditional farmhouses, and delve into the fascinating history of Bhutan as you wander through Paro's quaint streets. Don't forget to indulge in the local cuisine and experience the warm hospitality that Paro has to offer.

Thimphu

Welcome to Thimphu, the capital city of Bhutan and a vibrant dzongkhag bursting with charm and modernity. Nestled in the scenic Wang Chu Valley, Thimphu seamlessly blends Bhutan's rich cultural heritage with a progressive urban environment. Discover the magnificent Tashichho Dzong, embark on a pilgrimage to the National Memorial Chorten, or immerse yourself in the bustling local markets. From traditional festivals to contemporary art galleries, Thimphu offers a unique blend of traditional and contemporary experiences that will leave you enchanted.

Punakha

Embark on a journey to Punakha, a stunning dzongkhag located amidst the verdant valleys and terraced fields of Bhutan. Known for its historical significance as the former capital of Bhutan, Punakha holds a special place in the hearts of the Bhutanese people. Explore the grand Punakha Dzong, a majestic fortress where traditional monastic and administrative institutions harmoniously coexist. Surrounded by the gushing waters of Pho Chhu and Mo Chhu rivers, Punakha is also a gateway to picturesque hikes and breathtaking landscapes, making it a haven for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Wangdue Phodrang

Discover the beauty of Wangdue Phodrang, a captivating dzongkhag nestled at the foothills of the Black Mountains. As you explore its quaint towns and villages, you'll be captivated by the panoramic views of lush valleys, cascading waterfalls, and terraced rice fields. Wangdue Phodrang is famous for its serene Gangtey Valley, where you can witness the mesmerizing black-necked cranes during their migration. Dive deeper into Bhutanese culture with visits to age-old monasteries and experience the warm hospitality of the people. Wangdue Phodrang is a hidden gem that offers a perfect blend of untouched natural beauty and cultural heritage.

Bumthang

Step into the enchanting Bumthang, a spiritual dzongkhag that is often referred to as the "Sacred Valley of Bhutan." Known for its mystical aura and religious significance, Bumthang offers a unique spiritual experience. Explore the ancient temples and monasteries, such as Jambay Lhakhang and Kurjey Lhakhang, where legends and myths come alive. As you delve into the rich cultural heritage and traditions of Bumthang, allow yourself to be transported to a world of ancient legends and spiritual enlightenment. Surrounded by serene landscapes and pristine nature, Bumthang is a soul-stirring destination that will leave a lasting impression.
